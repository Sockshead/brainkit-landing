# BrainKit POC Personas

## Persona 1 — Marcus (Skeptic)
- **Name/Role:** Marcus, 47, independent strategy consultant — 12 active client engagements, 15 years solo
- **Tech level:** 2/5 — uses Notion, Google Workspace, reluctantly tried ChatGPT
- **Price anchor:** Pays $300-500/yr for Notion. Spent $8k on a VA last year.
- **Primary pain:** Re-explains context on every AI session. Has 4 active Notion workspaces that don't talk to each other.
- **Conversion trigger:** Sees a real before/after from someone with his exact workflow. Proof > features.
- **Red flags:** Anything that requires terminal, anything that could expose client data, vague promises, no real testimonials
- **Adversarial stance:** SKEPTIC — finds fatal flaws, assumes nothing works as described

## Persona 2 — Ana (Cost Optimizer)
- **Name/Role:** Ana, 38, bilingual executive coach (Spanish/English), Bogotá — 8 retainer clients, €3500/mo revenue
- **Tech level:** 2/5 — iPhone-first, Telegram power user, no laptop for most tasks
- **Price anchor:** Pays $97/mo for Calendly + Zoom + Loom bundle. Very price-conscious.
- **Primary pain:** Client session notes scattered across WhatsApp, Notion, voice memos. Takes 30 min to prep for each call.
- **Conversion trigger:** Understands the monthly maintenance value — that her system won't decay. Bilingual output matters.
- **Red flags:** Monthly fee without clear ROI, English-only output, requires Mac always-on (she travels), "setup call" she has to schedule
- **Adversarial stance:** COST OPTIMIZER — questions every price point, finds friction in the purchase flow

## Persona 3 — Tyler (Competitor-Aware)
- **Name/Role:** Tyler, 31, technical SaaS founder — seed stage, team of 6, already uses Claude Code daily
- **Tech level:** 5/5 — reads CLAUDE.md documentation, has tried Mem.ai, Notion AI, custom GPT wrappers
- **Price anchor:** Pays $20/mo Claude Pro. Considers $197 "lunch money" if it saves 2h/week.
- **Primary pain:** His CLAUDE.md is outdated. Started as a company doc, now it's a mess. Investor context, pilot customer details, team context not reflected.
- **Conversion trigger:** Understands the value immediately. Needs to believe Johannes's system is better than what he'd build himself in an afternoon.
- **Red flags:** "Isn't this just a CLAUDE.md template I could write myself?", no GitHub link, closed-source feel, can't inspect what he's getting
- **Adversarial stance:** COMPETITOR-AWARE — compares to free/DIY alternatives, finds parity gaps

## Persona 4 — Priya (Compliance/Trust)
- **Name/Role:** Priya, 44, independent management consultant — works with regulated industries (BFSI, healthcare), UK-based
- **Tech level:** 3/5 — uses Claude Code occasionally, Obsidian for notes, privacy-aware
- **Price anchor:** Has a legal budget for data compliance. Would pay more for an auditable, private solution.
- **Primary pain:** Can't use cloud AI tools with client data. Has to sanitize everything before pasting into Claude.
- **Conversion trigger:** Vault-on-Mac + private git repo = compliant local storage. If that's clearly explained, she converts.
- **Red flags:** "OpenAI Whisper API" mentioned in FAQ (sends voice through cloud), any ambiguity about who can see her vault, GitHub access by a third party
- **Adversarial stance:** COMPLIANCE/TRUST — finds trust gaps, security concerns, vague promises
